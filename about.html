Our Story

A slower way to stay in Ubud

An invitation to slow down, breathe deeply, and feel closer to nature.

Aruna Ubud Retreat was created as a peaceful hideaway for travelers who seek calm, comfort, and a deeper connection with Bali’s natural beauty. Surrounded by tropical greenery and warm textures, every space is shaped to feel quiet, personal, and close to the rhythm of Ubud.

Rooted in Calm

A place designed for quiet mornings and warm evenings.

From the first step into the garden to the final evening light, Aruna invites guests to experience Ubud at a slower pace. The retreat blends natural materials, soft surroundings, and thoughtful hospitality to create a stay that feels grounded, intimate, and restful.

Our Philosophy

A stay shaped by nature, comfort, and quiet details.

Aruna Ubud Retreat is guided by a simple idea: a place to stay should feel calm, personal, and connected to its surroundings. Every space is designed to bring guests closer to nature, while keeping the warmth and comfort of a private retreat.
